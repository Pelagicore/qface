QFace - A Generator Framework

Use a common IDL, bring your own generator. Inspired by Qt5.


Find Out More

QFace is a generator framework based on a modern IDL.

QFace is not a generator as such but enforces a common IDL format. It makes it very easy to build your own generator program to generate your custom code.
